# PharmaOS Comprehensive Evaluation Questionnaire

**Submitted by:** University Medical Center Procurement Division  
**Date:** March 2026  
**Purpose:** Full vendor assessment for enterprise pharmacy management system selection

---

## Questions

1. What is the overall system architecture of PharmaOS, and how do the different application layers interact?

2. How does the platform handle prescription refill tracking and authorization management?

3. What disaster recovery capabilities are implemented to restore system functionality after major disruptions?

4. Describe how medicine batch numbers and expiry dates are tracked within the inventory system.

5. What role-based access control mechanisms are implemented, and how are user permissions enforced?

6. Does PharmaOS provide real-time prescription monitoring dashboards accessible via mobile devices?

7. How does the system support barcode scanning for medication verification during dispensing?

8. What is the standard data retention period for prescription records and pharmacy transaction history?

9. How are payment transactions processed and recorded in the point-of-sale module?

10. What integration capabilities exist for connecting with external pharmaceutical supplier systems?

11. How does the platform generate alerts for medicines approaching expiration?

12. What authentication methods are supported, and does the system offer multi-factor authentication?

13. How can pharmacy administrators access consolidated reports across multiple pharmacy locations?

14. What mechanisms are used to monitor system performance and detect potential operational issues?

15. Does the platform support automated insurance eligibility verification during prescription processing?

16. How are operating theatre medication supplies tracked in hospital pharmacy workflows?

17. What audit logging capabilities exist for tracking user access to patient prescription records?

18. How does the system manage data lifecycle transitions from active records to archived storage?
