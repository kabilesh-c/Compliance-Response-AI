# PharmaOS Vendor Security and Operations Assessment

**Submitted by:** Metro Hospital Group  
**Date:** March 2026  
**Purpose:** Security and operational assessment for enterprise pharmacy system procurement

---

## Questions

1. How does PharmaOS implement role-based access control, and what are the standard user roles available in the system?

2. Describe how prescription records are stored and what information is captured during the dispensing process.

3. What mechanisms are in place to monitor medicine expiry dates and generate alerts for expiring stock?

4. How does the system log user activity, and what types of actions are recorded in audit trails?

5. Does PharmaOS support integration with hospital information systems for receiving electronic medication orders?

6. What security measures protect user authentication and session management?

7. How are controlled substance transactions tracked and documented within the platform?

8. What inventory reports are available to help pharmacy managers monitor stock levels and movements?

9. Does the platform provide AI-driven medication recommendation features to assist pharmacists with drug selection?

10. How does the system handle prescription refills, and what records are maintained for repeat prescriptions?

11. What integration methods are supported for connecting PharmaOS with external supplier systems?

12. How can administrators review historical prescription activity for compliance auditing purposes?
