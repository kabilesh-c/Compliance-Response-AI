# PharmaOS Enterprise Vendor Questionnaire

**Submitted by:** National Pharmacy Chain Inc.  
**Date:** March 2026  
**Purpose:** Comprehensive vendor evaluation for multi-location pharmacy deployment

---

## Questions

1. Describe the overall system architecture of PharmaOS and how the platform components interact.

2. How does the platform support prescription verification workflows before medication dispensing?

3. What backup procedures are implemented to protect pharmacy operational data?

4. Does PharmaOS support multi-location pharmacy operations, and how is inventory tracked across different branches?

5. What healthcare interoperability standards does the platform support for integration with clinical systems?

6. How are system events and user actions logged for audit and compliance purposes?

7. Does the platform support blockchain-based prescription tracking for enhanced security and traceability?

8. What types of analytics and reporting capabilities are available for monitoring pharmacy operations?

9. How does the system handle low stock alerts and reorder management for medicines?

10. What is the target system availability, and how does the platform ensure operational reliability?

11. How are patient prescription records retained, and what is the standard retention period?

12. Describe how the point-of-sale module processes pharmacy transactions and records payments.

13. What mechanisms allow administrators to review inventory transaction history?

14. How does PharmaOS manage inpatient department medication orders in hospital pharmacy environments?

15. What data export formats are supported for reporting and integration with external business systems?
