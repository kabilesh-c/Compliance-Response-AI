# PharmaOS Compliance and Audit Review Questionnaire

**Submitted by:** State Healthcare Compliance Office  
**Date:** March 2026  
**Purpose:** Compliance assessment for pharmacy management system audit capabilities

---

## Questions

1. What types of operational activities are recorded in the system audit logs?

2. How long are audit log records retained, and where are they stored?

3. How does the platform track prescription dispensing events for controlled substances?

4. What compliance reports can be generated from the system to support regulatory inspections?

5. How are inventory adjustments documented, and what information is captured in adjustment records?

6. Does the system support integration with insurance claim processing systems for automated billing?

7. How can administrators trace the sequence of actions related to a specific prescription record?

8. What mechanisms ensure that audit log records cannot be modified or deleted by system users?

9. How does the platform monitor system health and detect operational anomalies?

10. What data retention policies apply to prescription records and inventory transaction history?
